Two places:

1. Re-run the full suite yourself:


ssh awake@192.168.1.25
BENCH_DISK_DIR=/home/awake bash /home/awake/experiment_ble/cipher_tests/run_all.sh
Or just one script at a time:


ssh awake@192.168.1.25 '/home/awake/experiment_ble/cipher_tests/.venv/bin/python /home/awake/experiment_ble/cipher_tests/test_cipher.py'
ssh awake@192.168.1.25 '/home/awake/experiment_ble/cipher_tests/.venv/bin/python /home/awake/experiment_ble/cipher_tests/bench_cipher.py'
2. The benchmark CSV is on the RPi at /tmp/bench_cipher.csv — pull it to your laptop:


scp awake@192.168.1.25:/tmp/bench_cipher.csv /tmp/bench_cipher.csv
Then open it in Excel/LibreOffice or load in Python:


import pandas as pd
df = pd.read_csv('/tmp/bench_cipher.csv')
print(df.to_string())
The CSV has one summary row per benchmark/storage/payload combination with columns: benchmark, storage, payload_bytes, n, min_ms, mean_ms, p50_ms, p95_ms, p99_ms, max_ms, stdev_ms.

The scripts themselves live locally at experiment_ble/cipher_tests/ if you want to read or modify them before re-running.
